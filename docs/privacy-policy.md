# Privacy Policy for J.A.R.V.I.S Mobile

Effective date: 2026-04-15

J.A.R.V.I.S Mobile ("we", "our", or "us") provides a voice-enabled mobile assistant experience. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the app.

## Information We Collect

We may process the following categories of information to provide app functionality:

- Account and access information (for example, authentication token entered by you)
- Voice and text input you submit to the assistant
- Device permission data (such as microphone permission state)
- Technical and diagnostics data (for stability, troubleshooting, and service quality)

## How We Use Information

We use information to:

- Authenticate your access to backend services
- Process voice and text commands
- Return assistant responses and trigger requested actions
- Maintain service reliability, security, and abuse prevention

## Voice and AI Processing

Your commands may be sent to backend services and third-party AI providers configured by the service operator (for example, language model providers) to generate responses.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data may be shared with:

- Hosting/infrastructure providers required to run the service
- AI or workflow providers required to process commands
- Legal authorities when required by applicable law

## Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as needed for operational, security, and legal purposes, and then deleted or anonymized where feasible.

## Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational safeguards. No method of storage or transmission is 100% secure.

## Your Choices

You can:

- Revoke app permissions in device settings
- Stop using the app at any time
- Request deletion of data, subject to applicable legal requirements

## Children's Privacy

This app is not directed to children under 13 (or higher minimum age where required by local law).

## International Transfers

Data may be processed in countries other than your own, depending on hosting and provider infrastructure.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted with a revised effective date.
